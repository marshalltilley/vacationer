Packing & style

Island summer · 30 days
Weather
What to expect
  • Highs: mid 80s °F · trade-wind breeze
  • Lows: low 70s °F · warm nights
  • Volcanoes NP (4,000 ft): can drop to 50s
  • Brief afternoon showers possible
  • Pack one warm layer for Volcanoes NP + Mauna Kea / Manta night dive.
Direction
Style
  • Island casual — relaxed, sun-worn
  • Lightweight cotton + linen
  • Two "nicer" outfits for Waikiki dinners
  • Toa Luau night: aloha-shirt territory
  • Swimwear × 3 minimum (rotation, fast dry)
  • Sun hat + UPF rashguard
Footwear
Shoes
  • Reef sandals / Tevas · primary
  • Trail runners · Volcanoes NP + Pillbox + Kualoa
  • One nicer pair for Waikiki dinners
  • Water shoes for snorkeling rocks
  • Lava rock at Volcanoes NP eats soft soles — wear real shoes.
Pieces
Gear list
  • Reef-safe sunscreen (required on most beaches)
  • Polarized sunglasses
  • Snorkel mask (own one fits better than rentals)
  • Dry bag for kayak day
  • Refillable water bottle
  • Light rain shell · Volcanoes NP
  • Phone gimbal / GoPro for Manta night
  • Anna ballet kit (separate bag)
  • Golf attire + glove (clubs ship ahead)
