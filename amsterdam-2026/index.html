Executed Weather call paid off.

Haarlem + Zandvoort

How it actually went · phases in order
Morning
Transit to Haarlem
  • Bus 356 from Arena → Haarlem
  • Tap Apple Pay / contactless on board + exit (regional bus, NOT covered by GVB)
  • 356 both ways — also our ride home
Skipped morning coffee stops — went straight to bikes.
On arrival
Bike rental
  • Rent a Bike Haarlem · Lange Herenstraat 36
  • City bikes — perfect for the route
Went straight from the bus to bikes. Saved Haarlem walking for after the beach.
Core of the day
Ride · Nationaal Park Zuid-Kennemerland
  • Dedicated Dutch bike infrastructure
  • Forest sections → sand dunes → coastal scenery
  • Mostly flat, easy riding
  • Scenic cruise, not a workout
One of the trip highlights — the dunes ride through the national park.
Afternoon → dinner
Ubuntu Beach ★
  • Beach chairs + drinks (great drinks)
  • Read, nap, beach time
  • Dinner at Ubuntu — Mixed Starters ★ recommended
Stylish but relaxed, Ibiza / Bali energy. Earns the recommendation.
Evening
Bike back + Haarlem wander
  • Rode back through the dunes to Haarlem
  • Returned bikes
  • Walked Haarlem — Grote Markt, canals, side streets
Less touristy than Amsterdam — wealthy, historic, very walkable.
Dinner #2
Piccolo Forno
  • Pizza in Haarlem
Capped the day before bussing home.
Late evening
Transit home
  • Bus 356 back to Arena
Same bus both ways — no train transfer needed.
Packing for the day
Light kit
  • Sunglasses
  • Sunscreen
  • Light jacket or overshirt
  • Portable charger
  • Water bottle
  • North Sea wind cools fast after sunset.
